Frequently Asked Questions

Who will win The Hundred 2026?
MI London start as defending champions and slight favourites given their squad continuity and home ground advantage at the Kia Oval. Sunrisers Leeds, Birmingham Phoenix, and Trent Rockets are strong challengers. CricIntelligence AI will track live win probability for every match from July 21.
When does The Hundred 2026 start?
The Hundred 2026 starts Tuesday 21 July 2026. The opening match is MI London vs Sunrisers Leeds at the Kia Oval from 6:30pm BST. The tournament runs until Finals Day at Lord's on Sunday 16 August 2026.
How many balls is The Hundred?
Each team faces exactly 100 balls per innings. Bowlers bowl in 5-ball or 10-ball spells. There's a strategic timeout, power plays, and a similar feel to T20 but with a unique format that gives captains more tactical options around bowling changes.
What is different about MI London and Sunrisers Leeds?
MI London is the rebrand of the former Oval Invincibles, now linked with the Mumbai Indians franchise. Sunrisers Leeds replaced Northern Superchargers with ties to the Sunrisers Hyderabad IPL franchise. Manchester Originals became Manchester Super Giants.
Where is The Hundred 2026 Finals Day?
The Hundred 2026 Finals Day is at Lord's Cricket Ground, St John's Wood, London on Sunday 16 August 2026. Both the men's and women's finals are held on the same day. Eliminator matches are on Friday 14 August.
